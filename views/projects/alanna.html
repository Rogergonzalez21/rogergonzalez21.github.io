About the Project

Alanna is a tourism startup that looks to ease the work of travel agents around the world. The idea, is that you can access all the information of your agency wherever you are and whenever you need. You can check the information of your clients, previous travels, interests and likes.

Plus, you can create new travels with a lot of dedicated services and share the travel and itinerary information of the clients without leaving the platform.

Also, your clients can access and help you to set the information (like Visas & Passports. This is also working in mobile devices in Beta Testing.

What I did
I'm currently working full stack (Front-End and Back-End):
  • REST API built in Ruby on Rails: Auth & permissions with Devise, Devise token auth & Cancan.
  • A Web Application for the agents, built with AngularJS.
  • A second Web Application for the clients, built with AngularJS, where the client can check his data and modify it, or directly comunicate with his travel agent.
