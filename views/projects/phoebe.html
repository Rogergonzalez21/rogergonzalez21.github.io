About the Project

This is an app that allows photographers to upload pictures, sell them to clients and handle all the process until pictures are delivered (payment confirmation, print orders creation, in stock and delivery). The application is currently functioning and it is deployed in DigitalOcean

What I did
  • Various database improvements.
  • Full-Stack Development of the custom administration dashboard.
  • Deployment to Digital Ocean on Ubuntu Server.
